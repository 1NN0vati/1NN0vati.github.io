Our Mission

Empowering the next generation of technology innovators through education, collaboration, and entrepreneurship, fostering a culture of continuous learning, innovation, and impact.

Our Plan

To establish TISA as a premier technology innovation catalyst, extending our presence to empower a broader audience of young innovators. Through ongoing learning initiatives and strategic partnerships, we'll sustain our commitment to excellence, encouraging innovation and collaboration, and contributing to a tech-savvy, empowered generation.

Our Vision

Shaping a future where every young mind has the opportunity to lead technological innovation and create a better world through knowledge, creativity, and collaboration.

Our Team

Meet our dynamic and passionate team at TISA, comprised of visionary leaders, dedicated members, and enthusiastic students, all working together to drive innovation, promote technology, and inspire positive change in the educational and technological landscape.

Kulata Eric Allan

Chairperson

Kulata Eric Allan, the Chairperson of TISA, is dedicated to driving the organization's goals by fostering creativity, innovation, and knowledge-sharing. He expands TISA's reach, promotes innovation, and empowers youth through knowledge-enhancing events and TISA club support. Mr. Kulata Eric Allan advocates for entrepreneurship and collaboration, creating a dynamic technological ecosystem. His leadership is committed to TISA's mission and advancing technology and education.

Odongo Arthur

Prime Minister

Odongo Arthur serves as the Prime Minister of TISA, playing a pivotal role in the organization's leadership. With his dedication, he helps oversee various aspects of TISA's activities and projects. As a young and promising leader, he inspires students and youth to engage in technology, innovation, and entrepreneurship. Odongo Arthur's vision and commitment contribute to the growth and success of TISA clubs, promoting the organization's core values of knowledge-sharing, collaboration, and technological advancement. His role also extends to developing partnerships and encouraging TISA members to explore entrepreneurial opportunities, making a significant impact on the youth and tech communities.
